# Energy Anatomy in Javanese Tradition (Anatomi Energi Jawa)

## Traditional Energy Mapping (Pemetaan Energi)

### Javanese Text:
> "Chakra Jawa: Puser, dada, bathuk, pusar, sakral, suku.
> Meridian: Jalur energi ing awak.
> Titik tenaga: Pusat kekuatan ing badan."

### English Translation:
> "Javanese chakras: navel, chest, forehead, solar plexus, sacral, feet.
> Meridians: energy pathways in the body.
> Power points: Centers of strength in the body."

## Major Energy Centers (Pusat Energi Utama)

### 1. Suku (Feet Center)
- **Location:** Soles of feet
- **Function:** Grounding, stability
- **Properties:**
  - Earth connection
  - Power absorption
  - Stability anchor

### 2. Pusar (Navel Center)
- **Location:** Solar plexus
- **Function:** Power core
- **Properties:**
  - Energy reservoir
  - Personal power
  - Magnetic core

### 3. Jantung (Heart Center)
- **Location:** Heart region
- **Function:** Emotional power
- **Properties:**
  - Love force
  - Healing energy
  - Attraction point

### 4. Bathuk (Third Eye)
- **Location:** Forehead
- **Function:** Mental projection
- **Properties:**
  - Insight source
  - Magnetic gaze
  - Psychic vision
